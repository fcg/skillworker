新西兰过境签证 - Applying for a transit visa

过境签证要求申请人在新西兰机场海关内停留，且停留时间不超过24小时。申请流程也很简单，去新西兰签证中心申请即可。

TRANSIT VISA – GENERAL 过境新西兰——普通类别

Immigration New Zealand reserves the right to request additional information in the course of assessing an application and to retain information and documents on file. 新西兰移民局有权在申请审理过程中要求申请人提交补充材料，并有权保留申请人提交的信息及材料。

If you provide false or misleading information your application may be declined. 如果您在申请中提供虚假或误导性信息，申请将可能被拒签。

Who can be included in an application? 哪些申请人可被包含在同一份申请中？

  • An individual 单个申请人
  • A couple 夫妻
  • Parent(s) with dependant children under 20 applying at the same time for the same duration can apply together on one application form 父母偕同20岁以下未成年子女，且出行时间相同。

Non New Zealand citizens are required to hold a transit visa unless: 非新西兰公民如需过境新西兰，须申请新西兰过境签证，以下情况除外:

  • They hold a current New Zealand visa; or 过境人持有有效的新西兰签证；或
  • They are from a visa waiver country; or 过境人为免签证国家的公民；或
  • Their destination is Australia and they hold a current visa to enter Australia; or 过境人最终目的地为澳大利亚，并持有有效的澳大利亚入境签证；或
  • They are a citizen of one or more of the countries specified on the ‘transit visa country waiver list’ 过境人持有免过境签证国家的护照。

Transit Visa application form - INZ 1019 PDF 过境签证申请表Transit Visa application form - INZ 1019 PDF

Application fee申请费用 申请费用参照 http://www.immigration.govt.nz/branch/chinahomepage/fees/ 或 http://www.immigration.govt.nz/migrant/general/formsandfees/officeandfeescalculator/

PERSONAL DOCUMENTS 个人信息材料

Your current Passport(s) (original) 有效护照（原件）

A copy of the identity pages of the passports of every person included in the application 申请中包括的所有申请人的护照个人资料页复印件

Copy of Chinese identity card (CHINESE NATIONALS ONLY) 中国身分证副本(仅中国国民)

2 recent passport sized photos for each person included in the application, attached to the application form 申请中包含的每个申请人的近期护照照片2张，请粘贴于申请表格上

SUPPORTING DOCUMENTS支持材料

  • Airline ticket(s) and a confirmed travel itinerary飞机票以及已确认的行程安排
  • A statement of the purpose of the journey to the country of final destination (submitted in English)

个人解释说明，表明前往目的地国家的目的（须英文）。

另外新西兰为中国团体提供 group transit visa 。申请类似，区别见具体清单。

Applying for a group transit visa for chinese nationals

If you are a Chinese national and will be transiting New Zealand with a group, you may apply for a group transit visa. Your group transit visa will cover your group for a 24-hour stay within a New Zealand terminal.

详见新西兰移民局说明： http://www.immigration.govt.nz/migrant/stream/visit/transit/transitapplication/default.htm
